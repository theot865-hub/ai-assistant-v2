GOAL:
research Victoria BC realtors

PLAN:
Here is a short step-by-step plan to research Victoria BC realtors:

1. **Start with online searches**: Open a web browser and search for "Victoria BC realtors" or "real estate agents in Victoria BC" to get a list of results.
2. **Check online directories**: Visit online directories like Realtor.ca, VictoriaRealEstate.com, or CanadaRealEstate.com to find a list of licensed realtors in Victoria.
3. **Read online reviews**: Look up review websites like Google Reviews, Yelp, or Houzz to see what past clients have to say about their experience with specific realtors.
4. **Check professional associations**: Visit the websites of professional associations like the Victoria Real Estate Board (VREB) or the Real Estate Council of British Columbia (RECBC) to find a list of licensed realtors in Victoria.
5. **Contact a few top choices**: Reach out to 2-3 realtors who have caught your attention and ask them questions about their experience, services, and commission rates.
